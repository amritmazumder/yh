The Debate

Sparked by an opaque document produced by the TRAI, the debate around Net Neutrality in India has pitted the people and the telecom operators against each other. Some have argued that TRAI's chosen method to receive consultation on the matter was designed to confuse, and have questioned the regulatory body's primary interests (which should be to protect the consumer.)
