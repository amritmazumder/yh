The Basics
What is Net Neutrality?

Once you pay for the internet, you should be able to:

Access any website on the internet
Experience all websites equally.
Pay for how much you use.
How does a violation of Net Neutrality affect me?
As an Internet User
As an Internet Startup
You can only access websites selected by your TSP.
All other Websites run slower.
Instead of paying for how much, you pay for what you use.
You will not be able to compete with the status quo.
Telecom companies will be able to decide your growth.
All your decisions will depend on the Telecom Companies.
