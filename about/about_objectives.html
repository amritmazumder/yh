Objectives
To Collect

Yellow Hats draw from the myriad of published resources & projects on the web in order to develop a comprehensive reading of an issue. Be it a news article from a leading publication, a personal blog post, or a comment on a YouTube video, or a meme on 4Chan: the Hats connect these varied entities into a single conversation.

To Organize

In a response to the overwhelming amount of information available on the internet on any hot topic, Yellow Hats structure and organize this information into a clear sequence of events that is easy to follow and keep up with.

To Clarify

The Hats draw from this information to develop informational, interactive and participatory experiences. Through this process, the Hats are able to distill and deconstruct any issue into a tiered experience that is valuable to both invested activists and casual browsers.
