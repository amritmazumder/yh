Values
Balance

Yellow Hat is dedicated to awareness and education. This invokes a responsibility to present all perspectives on any issue. While designing and editing may be inherently political, Yellow Hat as a community should remain apolitical and accommodate different perspectives.

Timing

Yellow Hat must never be isolated from the larger public discourse. Yellow Hat projects should look to capitalize upon popular cultural moments and tropes in an attempt to create an entry into a conversation about techno-politics.

Context

Yellow Hat must use the web browser as a venue for its activities. By occupying a device—agnostic space, Yellow Hat enables universal access, congruent to Yellow Hat’s mission to enable access to techno-political conversations.

Depth

Yellow Hat must recognize and cater to the attention span of the average internet browser. Through its projects and materials, Yellow Hat must be able to communicate to an audience with different levels of enthusiasm.
